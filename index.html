2) Optional: paste your current holdings

If you paste holdings, the engine will output trades vs what you already own.
Format: ticker,shares,avgCost,lastTradeDate. Headers optional. Dates optional.
Don’t paste anything? No problem. You’ll still get the target allocation. Trades panel will be “buy list” style.

3) Generate the plan

Click once. Wait. Then execute the suggested trades in your broker.
Sequence: Setup → (optional holdings) → Generate → Execute trades → Re-run on the next cadence.
This tool is a decision assistant, not a broker. No guarantees. Real markets are messy. Use position sizing and don’t YOLO.
